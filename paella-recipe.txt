Ingredients:
 - arborio rice 250grams 
 - 1tsp smoked paprika
 - tin of chopped tomatoes
 - 1 1/2 pint chicken stock
 - chicken thighs or breast fillets, cubed
 - prawns, seafood selection
 - peas
 - chopped onion

Directions:
 1. In paella pan, sweat off the onions, and add the chicken
 2. add tomato, paprika, rice and a small amount of stock, cook for a few minutes
 3. add more stock as cooking
 4. add prawns and peas later on and cook until the stock is absorbed
